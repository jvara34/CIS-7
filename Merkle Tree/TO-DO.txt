** This program will get 4 different inputs and create a merkle tree with that data


- Hash each L1, L2... by themselves 
- Add L1 and L2 together and L3 and L4 together 
    - hash L1+L2 and L3+L4 indendntly 
- Then add the hash(L1+L2) and hash(L3+L4) 
    - This creates the top hash 

- Print out everytime you hash something and label it per the picture on wikipedia
